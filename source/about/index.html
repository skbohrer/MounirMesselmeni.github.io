My name is Andrey Paramonov. On the web I'm also known as ndpar, which, by the way, is pronounced as "aNDy Par".

I live in Toronto, Canada with my beautiful wife and two wonderful kids.

I used to be a professional mathematician and hobbyist programmer, but at some point those two swapped. These days I earn my bread and butter by writing web applicaitons for JVM platform. At my spare time I indulge in functional programming with Erlang and Lisp.

I love learning new stuff, I like asking questions, and somewhere deep inside my heart I remain a mathematician who strives for rigor even in everyday life, which sometimes annoys people around me.

My favourite social network is GitHub. I also use Twitter and LinkedIn.

If you want to contact me, feel free to send me an email to blog at ndpar.com.
